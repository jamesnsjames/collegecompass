MES T.O. Abdulla Memorial College, Kunnukara

  • About the College: MES T.O. Abdulla Memorial College, Kunnukara, is a self-financing institution affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. Established in 2004, it is strategically located between Aluva, Angamali, and North Paravoor.

  • Campus & Location: The lush green campus offers a peaceful academic environment, with proximity to the Periyar River and the Cochin International Airport, making it easily accessible.

  • Academics & Courses: The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in streams such as **Commerce, Computer Science, Management, and Arts**. The curriculum is designed to meet industry standards, ensuring holistic student development.

  • Facilities: MES Kunnukara provides **modern classrooms, a well-equipped library, computer labs, and sports facilities**. The institution also offers hostel accommodation for students from distant locations.

  • Student Life & Activities: The college encourages extracurricular activities through **cultural fests, tech clubs, and NSS programs**, helping students develop leadership and teamwork skills.

  • Placements & Career Support: The dedicated placement cell collaborates with reputed companies, ensuring students receive career guidance and job opportunities.
