• Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering (SNGCE), established in 2002, is a premier self-financing institution dedicated to providing quality engineering and management education.

  • The institution is managed by the Sree Narayana Gurukulam Charitable Trust, a recognized charitable society with eminent members from various fields across the globe.

  • SNGCE operates under the guidance of SNDP Kunnathunadu Union, ensuring a strong academic and ethical foundation.

  • The college offers state-of-the-art facilities, modern infrastructure, and a research-driven learning environment to its students.

  • SNGCE has a reputation for excellence in engineering and management education, producing skilled professionals ready to excel in their careers.
