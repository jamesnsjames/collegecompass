• S.N.M. College Maliankara symbolizes the steady advancement of Moothakunnam, transforming from an underdeveloped village into a center of social and cultural regeneration. The college follows the vision of Sree Narayana Guru: "Emancipation Through Education".

  • Founded in 1964: The institution was inaugurated by Sri Sahodaran Ayyappan, an ardent disciple of Sree Narayana Guru.

  • Academic Growth: Degree Programs started in 1972, followed by Postgraduate Programs in 1983.

  • Current Offerings: 13 Undergraduate Programs, 8 Postgraduate Programs, and 1 Integrated Program, with a total of 1,692 students.

  • Research Excellence: Since 2011, two Research Centers for Doctoral Programs in Botany and Chemistry have been operational.

  • NAAC Accreditation: In 2024, re-accredited in the III Cycle with an "A" grade and a CGPA of 3.08.
