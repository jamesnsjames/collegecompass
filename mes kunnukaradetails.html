• MES T.O. Abdulla Memorial College, Kunnukara is a self-financing college affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University Kottayam. Established in 2004, the college is located in the middle of the towns of Aluva, Angamali and North Paravoor.

  • The campus which spreads out in a vast stretch of lush greenery is a sure sight of delight for a lover of nature. The college is also in close vicinity to river Periyar and International Airport, Nedumbassery, Kochi.
